Jeddah Economic City

Lined strategically along the coastline of the Red Sea, Jeddah Economic City is design to embody a new point of view in what a mega city is all about. The City in mind is planned to breathe life into the Northern area of Jeddah, attracting investors, tourists, as well as business opportunities that will break global records, becoming the first of its kind in city life..

Recreation

Recreation underlines the dynamism of Jeddah Economic City, playing a vital role in defining the unique vision and surpassing expectations to all.

Old Town

One of the character areas specially mandated for shopping includes Old Town, a unique shopping district that draws the inspiration behind its design from the many global destinations for shoppers and visitors.

Resort Living

With a well-organized landscape grid system, JEC is a distinct project that takes great value in nurturing a distinguished lifestyle and living facilities to the modern age.

Financial Street

Thriving to be the leading smart city in the world, the JEC is visualized to be a nestling demography for world-class office parks, equipped with a landscape to inspire creativity to executives, employees as well as business leaders.

Business District

One of the impressive elements in the Jeddah Economic City is the aligned business towers which are to be strategically placed within a brilliant conclave.

Water edge

Jeddah Economic City will feature a magnificent man-made waterfront with an area of over 90,000 square meters surrounded with walkways, cafe's, shops, and restaurants.
