Hello, I'm Damian, 24 years old self-taught photographer. I have been photographing since 2012. I am constantly exploring the secrets of photography, because it's my passion


started with sports photography, which I learned as a teenager at school tournaments and Legionowo sports fields. In 2014 I was able to fulfill my first photographic dream. I received my first media accreditation. Since then, I have made photos on hundreds of matches, also abroad.


Fascination with Warsaw, so photography of landscape and architecture, was initiated by cooperation with the Nowa Warszawa portal. I gained the opportunity to photograph Warsaw from places inaccessible to ordinary passers-by. If you've ever seen my photos, it's very likely you associate me with photos of Warsaw from above.
