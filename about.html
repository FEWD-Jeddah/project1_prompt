Basic Health & Care Tips to Keep Your Cat Healthy

  1. Groom your cat regularly . Whether your cat has short or long fur, he will benefit greatly from regular brushing
  2. Provide fresh water daily
  3. Make sure you have enough litter boxes
  4. Train your cat to use a scratching post
  5. Keep your cat's teeth clean
  6. Spay or neuter your cat
